# RealismThrift Export Co., Ltd. — Company Introduction

## 🌍 About RealismThrift
RealismThrift Export Co., Ltd. is a premier global wholesale exporter of high-quality **used brand clothes, shoes, and bags**. Headquartered in **Huizhou, China**, we operate one of the region's largest and most efficient sorting facilities, serving over **1,000 wholesale buyers** across **100+ countries**.

Founded in **2012**, we have spent over a decade perfecting the art of "First-Pick" sourcing and A-Grade quality control, establishing ourselves as a trusted partner for wholesalers in Africa, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and the Americas.

## 🔍 Quality Is Our Signature
We maintain an industry-leading **80%+ customer reorder rate** through a rigorous quality assurance process:
1.  **Strategic Sourcing**: We collect raw materials exclusively from high-income urban areas in China's first-tier cities (**Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Chengdu**) to ensure fashion relevance and minimal wear.
2.  **6-Point QC Check**: Every item undergoes a strict inspection to ensure:
    - No holes, stains, or fading.
    - Functional zippers and hardware.
    - Integrity of soles and linings.
    - Brand authenticity and stylistic appeal.
3.  **A-Grade Standards**: Our products are sorted and graded to meet the highest market demands, ensuring our buyers can build a solid reputation in their local markets.
