# RealismThrift Export Co., Ltd. Company Introduction

## About RealismThrift

RealismThrift Export Co., Ltd. is a wholesale exporter of used brand clothes, used brand shoes, and used bags from China. The company is based in Huizhou, Guangdong, and works with overseas buyers who need practical sourcing, grading, packing, and export support.

RealismThrift serves wholesale buyers in markets including Africa, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Latin America, and other regions where second-hand fashion has strong resale demand.

## Facility and Operations

The company's operations are built around category sorting and export preparation.

- Facility: Sorting and warehouse operations in Huizhou, Guangdong.
- Categories: Used clothes, used shoes, and used bags.
- Workflow: Sourcing, sorting, quality inspection, packing, documentation, and shipment coordination.
- Loading support: Container planning for 20ft and 40ft orders.

## Sourcing Approach

RealismThrift prioritizes urban raw material channels in major Chinese cities. First-pick sourcing can improve the chance of better style freshness, lower wear, and stronger brand potential, but it does not mean every item in a mixed wholesale batch is branded.

The company communicates this boundary clearly because used-goods buyers need realistic expectations before confirming an order.

## Quality Standard

RealismThrift defines A-grade as sellable used quality, not new-goods quality. A-grade products may show light signs of previous use, but they should remain clean, functional, wearable, and suitable for resale.

Inspection points include:

- Clothing: holes, heavy stains, fading, fabric condition, buttons, zippers, and shape.
- Shoes: outsole wear, upper condition, lining, odor, pair matching, and size mix.
- Bags: zippers, straps, corners, lining, hardware, style appeal, and obvious counterfeit-risk removal.

## Product Categories

- Used Brand Clothes: Mixed clothing selections for wholesale resale channels.
- Used Brand Shoes: Sneakers, casual shoes, sandals, boots, and other market-suitable footwear.
- Used Brand Bags: Handbags, backpacks, shoulder bags, crossbody bags, and travel bags.
